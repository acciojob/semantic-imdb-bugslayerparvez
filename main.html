Summary

In the kingdom of Hirak, a ruthless ruler exploits the people and the famed twin singers Goopy and Bagha return to challenge his tyranny. Using wit, music, and the help of oppressed subjects, they plot to overthrow the cruel regime and restore freedom to the land.
